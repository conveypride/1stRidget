About Us

Ridget Construction is a dynamic and innovative construction company dedicated to shaping the skylines and communities we serve. Established with a vision to redefine construction excellence, we have become synonymous with quality, reliability, and a client-centric approach.

Founding Principles


Founded on the principles of integrity, craftsmanship, and a passion for building. 1st Ridget Construction was established by a team of industry veterans with decades of combined experience. Our founders envisioned a construction company that not only delivers exceptional results but also values relationships with clients, partners, and the communities in which we operate.

Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and a commitment to ethical business practices.

Excellence: We pursue excellence in every aspect of our work, from project planning to construction execution.

Collaboration: We foster strong partnerships with clients, architects, subcontractors, and suppliers to ensure seamless project delivery.

Innovation: Embracing technological advancements and creative solutions, we continuously seek innovative approaches to construction challenges.

Our Aspirations


Our primary aspiration is to be at the forefront of innovation within the construction sector. We aim to embrace and integrate cutting-edge technologies, sustainable practices, and creative solutions that not only enhance the efficiency of our projects but also contribute to the overall advancement of the industry.

Our Philosophy

Recognizing our responsibility to the environment and future generations, we embrace sustainable practices. Our philosophy emphasizes the integration of eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient solutions, and environmentally conscious construction methods. We are committed to minimizing our ecological footprint and promoting a greener, more sustainable built environment. Construction is an ever-evolving industry, and our philosophy encourages a mindset of continuous improvement. We actively seek opportunities to learn, adapt, and grow. By staying informed about industry trends, advancements, and best practices, we ensure that our team remains dynamic and capable of meeting the evolving needs of our clients and the construction landscape.
